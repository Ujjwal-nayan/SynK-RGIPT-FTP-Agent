â“ COMMON FIRST-TIME QUESTIONS

Q: Where are my files saved?
A: By default in: ~/College Material/
   Each course gets its own folder with the exact FTP name.

Q: How often does it sync?
A: Every 60 minutes automatically.

Q: Does it use my internet data?
A: No! It only works on RGIPT WiFi and doesn't need Cyberoam login.

Q: Can I add multiple courses?
A: Yes! Just repeat Step 3 for each subject.

Q: Will it overwrite my notes?
A: YES! Do NOT save personal notes inside synced PDFs.
   Keep notes in a separate folder.

Q: How do I stop it?
A: Terminal: systemctl --user stop synk.service
